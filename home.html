Introduction

Khushi Arora (born July 17, 2000) is a graduate in Bachelor of Business Administration. Her life takes inspiration from her personal experiences and has received critical praise from her closed ones.

Based in Ahmedabad, Gujarat, Khushi joined Ahmedabad University in 2019 to become a skilled individual in Business Administration. Her commendable career includes successfully organizing and managing Aarambh: Experience the Market (2019) which is a three day-long event by putting up stalls in the flea market, selling a range of products and services like apparels, wearable accessories, stationery, books, intellectual games, plants and saplings and mobile apps.
